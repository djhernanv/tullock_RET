Opportunity for Higher Piece Rate


In the first of the next {{ Constants.num_rounds|apnumber}} rounds all participants will have the same piece rate of {{ Constants.tokensper_string}} Token per correctly solved sequence.

In all the following rounds exactly one out of the {{ Constants.players_per_group|apnumber }} group members will get a higher piece rate of {{ Constants.tokensper_string_high }} Tokens per correct sequence. The other players will keep their piece rate of {{ Constants.tokensper_string }} Token per sequence. How the person that gets the high piece rate is chosen is explained below.

All participants in all rounds will get 1 Token per {{ Constants.secondsper_token }} seconds spent in the switch mode.


Investment for a Higher Piece Rate


{% if Constants.treatment %}

To obtain the higher piece rate you will be able to invest some of your available income, i.e. your income from the letter counting task after taxation and redistribution. Note that you can only invest income received in the current round and you cannot invest earnings from the switch mode.

{% else %} To obtain the higher piece rate you will have the opportunity to invest some of your earnings from counting "a"s in the Letter Counting Task. Note that you can only invest income received in the current round and you cannot invest earnings from the switch mode. {% endif %}

Your probability of obtaining the higher piece rate is equal to the share of your investment relative to the sum of all investments in your group (including yours). You can keep the tokens that you do not invest, but your investment will not be refunded, even if you do not obtain the higher piece rate. The more you invest, the higher are your chances!

Therefore, participant Y has a 40% probability of obtaining the high piece rate in the next round.
